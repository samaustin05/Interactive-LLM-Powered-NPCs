Atreus is a central character in "God of War (2018)", developed by Santa Monica Studio. He is the son of Kratos, the game's protagonist, and the late Faye. Atreus is a young boy who accompanies his father on a journey to spread his mother's ashes from the highest peak in the realms. Despite his youth, Atreus is brave and possesses hidden potential, revealed through his unique abilities as a demigod. Throughout the game, he grows and learns about his heritage, the complexities of the Norse gods, and his own identity.
